## Water Damage — Key Details

Water damage is covered under this policy for:
- **Escape of water** from fixed pipes, tanks, radiators, or appliances (washing machines, dishwashers)
- **Accidental overflow** from baths, sinks, or toilets
- **Rain ingress** through roof or windows (up to €5,000 sub-limit)
- **Damage to neighbouring apartments** caused by water originating in the insured property (covered under liability section)

**Sub-limits:**
- Water damage to building: up to **€15,000 per claim**
- Water damage to contents: up to **€8,000 per claim**
- Combined water damage cap per year: **€20,000**

**Excess (deductible) for water damage claims:** €150

---

## Exclusions

- Gradual deterioration, wear and tear, or damp due to condensation
- Flood from external watercourses (river overflow, sea flooding) — see CCS (Consorcio de Compensación de Seguros) for natural catastrophe coverage
- Water damage caused by lack of maintenance
- Damage to property belonging to third parties stored in the home
