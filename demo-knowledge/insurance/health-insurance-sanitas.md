## Exclusions

The following are **not covered** under this policy:

1. **Cosmetic or aesthetic surgery** — including rhinoplasty, breast augmentation, liposuction, and any procedure performed solely for cosmetic purposes.
2. **Pre-existing dental conditions** — dental treatment for conditions diagnosed or treated prior to policy inception (January 1, 2023). Routine dental check-ups are not covered under this policy; dental cover requires a separate add-on.
3. **Experimental treatments** — treatments not approved by the Spanish Agency of Medicines (AEMPS).
4. **Obesity treatment** — weight-loss surgery or pharmacological weight-loss programs.
5. **Gender reassignment surgery** — not covered under current plan.
6. **Fertility treatments (IVF)** — not included in base plan; available as optional add-on.
7. **Injuries from professional sports** — injuries sustained while participating in professional or semi-professional sporting competition.
8. **Self-inflicted injuries.**

*Note: Pre-existing conditions disclosed at underwriting are covered after a 12-month waiting period.*
