## Exclusions

The following are **not covered** under this policy:

### Sports and Activities

1. **Adventure sports** — bungee jumping, skydiving, paragliding, base jumping, mountaineering above 4,000m, white-water rafting (grade IV+), and any motorised racing.
2. **Extreme sports** — any activity classified as extreme or high-risk at point of booking.
3. **Professional sporting competition** — injuries sustained while competing professionally.
4. **Skiing and snowboarding** — not included in base policy; available as €49/year add-on (Winter Sports).
5. **Scuba diving** deeper than 30m or without a recognised qualification.

### Medical Exclusions

1. **Pre-existing medical conditions** — any condition diagnosed, treated, or for which medication was prescribed in the 12 months prior to the trip start date. This includes but is not limited to: cardiovascular conditions, diabetes, cancer, respiratory conditions, and mental health conditions requiring ongoing treatment.
2. **Pregnancy beyond 28 weeks** — complications arising after the 28th week of pregnancy are not covered.
3. **Elective treatment abroad** — treatment sought intentionally abroad (medical tourism).
4. **Dental treatment** (non-emergency) — routine or cosmetic dental work abroad.

### Other Exclusions

- Travel to countries under Spanish Foreign Ministry "Do Not Travel" advisory
- Losses due to drug or alcohol intoxication
- War, civil unrest, or terrorism (CCS coverage applies for Spain only)
- Business equipment or samples (separate commercial policy required)

## How to Make a Claim

1. **Medical emergency:** Call **900 123 456** (free, 24h) immediately. Do not pay and claim back for hospitalisation — call first.
2. **Non-emergency claims:** Submit within 30 days of return via mapfre.es/clientes or app.
3. **Required documents:** Police report (for theft), medical reports, receipts, boarding passes.
4. **Reference format:** MAP-CLM-XXXXXXXX

## Important Notes

- Policy applies to trips departing from Spain only.
- Insured must be resident in Spain at time of policy purchase.
- Coverage does not apply if travel advice against the destination was in effect at time of booking.
- USA/Canada medical supplement (included): sub-limit of €500,000 for emergency medical costs given higher healthcare costs in North America.
