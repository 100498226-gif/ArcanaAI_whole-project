# New Hire Onboarding Guide — InfiniteWatch SL

**Version:** 3.2  
**Last updated:** September 2024  
**Owner:** People Operations  
**Applies to:** All new hires, permanent and contractor roles

---

## Overview

This guide covers the 8-step onboarding process for new team members at InfiniteWatch. All steps must be completed within the first 2 weeks. The onboarding buddy assigned at offer acceptance is responsible for ensuring each step is completed on time.

---

## Step 1 — Laptop Setup

**Timeline:** Day 1, before 10:00  
**Owner:** IT (it-support@horizonanalytics.com)

- IT will have your MacBook Pro configured and waiting at your desk on Day 1.
- Log in with your corporate credentials (provided via encrypted email the evening before).
- Install required software from the Horizon Self-Service Portal: Slack, Zoom, 1Password, VS Code, Notion, Jira.
- Enable FileVault disk encryption and set a screen lock (max 5 minutes idle).
- Connect to the corporate VPN (Horizon-VPN) using the credentials in 1Password.

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Laptop received and logged into
- [ ] FileVault enabled
- [ ] VPN configured and connected
- [ ] Self-service apps installed

---

## Step 2 — GitHub Access

**Timeline:** Day 1, afternoon  
**Owner:** Engineering lead

- Submit access request via the #it-requests Slack channel with your GitHub username.
- You will be added to the `horizon-analytics` GitHub organisation within 2 hours.
- Clone the main repositories: `horizon-core`, `horizon-api`, `horizon-docs`.
- Confirm you can run `make dev` in `horizon-core` without errors.

**Checklist:**
- [ ] GitHub org invitation accepted
- [ ] Repositories cloned
- [ ] Dev environment running locally

---

## Step 3 — Notion Access

**Timeline:** Day 1  
**Owner:** People Operations

- Invitation sent to corporate email automatically on Day 1.
- Key spaces to bookmark: Team Wiki, Project Tracker, Client Directory, HR Handbook.
- Read the Engineering Handbook (Notion > Team Wiki > Engineering) before Day 3.

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Notion invitation accepted
- [ ] Engineering Handbook read

---

## Step 4 — 1:1 with Direct Lead

**Timeline:** Day 1 or Day 2 (30-minute meeting)  
**Owner:** Direct manager

Topics to cover:
- Role expectations and first 90-day goals
- Team dynamics and key stakeholders
- Immediate priorities for the first two weeks
- Communication preferences and working style
- Questions from the new hire

---

## Step 5 — Shadow Week

**Timeline:** Days 2–5  
**Owner:** Onboarding buddy

- Attend all team standups and relevant meetings as an observer.
- Shadow the onboarding buddy on at least one client call or internal project meeting.
- Read through the last 3 monthly team retrospectives in Notion.
- Review the current sprint in Jira and understand in-flight work.
- Shadow a production deployment (read-only access only in first week).

---

## Step 6 — HR and Compliance

**Timeline:** By end of Week 1  
**Owner:** People Operations

- Complete the mandatory compliance training modules in the Horizon LMS (approx. 2 hours):
  - GDPR and Data Protection
  - Information Security Policy
  - Code of Conduct
  - Anti-Bribery and Corruption
- Sign and return the following (via DocuSign):
  - Employment contract (if not already signed)
  - Confidentiality and NDA agreement
  - Acceptable Use Policy

---

## Step 7 — First Task Assignment

**Timeline:** Day 3 (no later than Day 5)  
**Owner:** Direct lead

- Lead assigns a scoped, well-defined first ticket from the backlog.
- Ticket should be completable within 3–5 days without deep institutional knowledge.
- Goal: familiarise with the codebase, CI/CD pipeline, and code review process.
- First PR must be reviewed by the onboarding buddy within 24 hours of submission.

---

## Step 8 — 2-Week Check-in

**Timeline:** End of Day 10  
**Owner:** Direct lead + People Operations

30-minute meeting covering:
- How onboarding went (what worked, what was confusing)
- Whether 90-day goals need adjustment
- Any blockers or concerns
- Feedback on the onboarding process itself (anonymous survey link sent separately)
