## Milestone Definitions

### Milestone 1 — Kickoff (€15,000)
Triggered by: execution of this agreement and attendance of the project kickoff meeting (scheduled December 3, 2024).  
Deliverables at this stage: project plan, technical architecture document, design mockups (first draft).

### Milestone 2 — UAT Delivery (€20,000)
Triggered by: delivery of a functional system to Client X's test environment for UAT.  
Deliverables: fully functional dashboard, all integrations connected, test documentation, UAT test scripts.  
Expected delivery: **March 14, 2025**.

### Milestone 3 — Final Delivery (€10,000)
Triggered by: written sign-off from Client X on completion of UAT, resolution of all P1 and P2 defects, and successful deployment to production.  
Deliverables: production deployment, handover documentation, 30-day warranty period begins.  
Expected delivery: **April 30, 2025**.

## Out of Scope

The following are explicitly excluded from the fixed price:

1. Infrastructure hosting costs (Client X's responsibility; Chillón Consulting will advise)
2. Third-party licences or API costs
3. More than 2 rounds of design revisions per component
4. Training sessions beyond the included 4-hour handover session
5. Support after the 30-day warranty period (separate retainer agreement required)

---

## Warranty

A **30-day warranty** applies after final acceptance. During this period, Chillón Consulting will fix any defects at no additional charge, excluding defects caused by:
- Client-side changes to data sources or infrastructure
- Third-party service outages
- Features explicitly marked as out of scope
