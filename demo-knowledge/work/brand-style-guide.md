# Brand Style Guide — Chillón Consulting

**Version:** 1.4  
**Last updated:** October 2024  
**Owner:** Ignacio Chillón

---

## Brand Overview

Chillón Consulting is a boutique technology and product consultancy based in Barcelona. Our brand conveys: **precision, clarity, and trustworthiness** — modern but not trendy; human but not casual.

---

## Logo

- **Primary logo:** Wordmark "Chillón Consulting" in Montserrat ExtraBold
- **Icon mark:** Stylised "M" monogram (for use on small surfaces: favicons, profile pictures)
- **Clear space:** Minimum 20px clear space on all sides of the logo
- **Minimum size:** 120px wide (digital); 30mm wide (print)
- **Do not:** Stretch, recolour, add drop shadows, or place on busy backgrounds

**Logo files:** Available in SVG (preferred), PNG (transparent), and PDF formats  
**File location:** Brand assets > logos/

## Typography

### Primary Typeface: Montserrat

| Weight | Use |
|---|---|
| **Montserrat Bold (700)** | Social media posts, display headlines, key callouts |
| Montserrat SemiBold (600) | Section headings (H2, H3), slide titles |
| Montserrat Regular (400) | Body text in presentations and web |

### Secondary Typeface: Georgia (Serif)

- Use only in long-form written documents (proposals, reports)
- Body text at 11pt / 1.5 line height in PDF documents
- Not used in digital/web contexts

### Code / Monospace: JetBrains Mono

- For any technical documentation, code blocks, or data tables

### Web Stack (if Google Fonts not available)

- Fallback: `'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif`

---

## Tone of Voice

- **Professional but not corporate** — avoid jargon, write in plain English/Spanish
- **Direct** — lead with the key point; don't bury the takeaway
- **Specific** — use numbers and facts rather than vague superlatives
- **First person** — "we" for the consultancy; "I" in personal communications from Ignacio

## Social Media

### LinkedIn

- **Post font:** Montserrat Bold for any designed graphics
- **Colour:** Brand Blue #1A6BFF backgrounds or Dark Navy with white text
- **Image dimensions:** 1200×627px (link preview), 1080×1080px (square post)
- **Hashtags:** No more than 3 hashtags per post; always in Spanish unless audience is explicitly international
- **Posting frequency:** 2–3 times per week

### Twitter / X

- Text-only preferred for thought leadership content
- Graphics: same as LinkedIn dimensions for the 1:1 format

## What Not to Do

- Do not use Comic Sans, Papyrus, or Times New Roman
- Do not use more than 2 typefaces in a single document
- Do not use gradients on the logo
- Do not use clipart or stock photos of people shaking hands
- Do not put the logo on a blue background (use white or dark navy only)
- Do not use ALL CAPS for body text (headers only)
