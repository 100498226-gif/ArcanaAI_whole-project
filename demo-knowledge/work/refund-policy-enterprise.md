# Refund Policy — Enterprise Plan

**Montoya Consulting**  
Version: 2.1 — Effective from October 1, 2024

---

## Scope

This policy applies to all Enterprise plan engagements and software licences sold by Montoya Consulting to corporate clients. It supersedes all prior versions.

---

## Standard Refund Terms

### Enterprise Plan (Annual Contract)

| Period | Refund Entitlement |
|---|---|
| Within 30 days of contract start | **Full refund** — 100% of fees paid |
| Day 31 to end of contract year | **Pro-rata refund** — refund calculated on remaining unused full months |
| After contract year 1 (renewals) | Pro-rata refund on remaining unused full months |

**Example:** A client on a €24,000/year Enterprise plan cancels on day 45. Refund = (11 months remaining / 12 months) × €24,000 = €22,000. The first partial month (15 days used of month 2) is not refunded.

---

## Non-Refundable Items

The following fees are **non-refundable under any circumstances**:

1. **Onboarding fee** — the one-time onboarding and implementation fee (typically €2,500–€5,000 depending on scope) is non-refundable once onboarding has commenced.
2. **Custom development** — any bespoke development work invoiced separately under a statement of work.
3. **Training sessions** — delivered training (in-person or virtual) is non-refundable once the session has taken place.
4. **Data migration fees** — non-refundable once migration has been initiated.
5. **Third-party costs** — any costs passed through from third-party vendors (cloud infrastructure, data providers, API integrations) that have already been committed.

---

## Cancellation Process

1. Client submits written cancellation notice to: contracts@montoyaconsulting.com
2. Notice must include: contract reference, company name, requested cancellation date.
3. Cancellation takes effect on the last day of the current billing month (minimum 5 working days' notice required).
4. Refund is processed within **14 working days** of confirmed cancellation.
5. Refunds are issued to the original payment method (bank transfer or credit card, as applicable).

---

## Exceptions and Disputes

- Refund requests outside normal policy require written approval from the Managing Director.
- In the event of a dispute, the parties agree to attempt resolution through mediation before escalating to legal proceedings.
- Governing law: Spanish law. Jurisdiction: Courts of Barcelona.

---

## Service Credits (Alternative to Refund)

At the client's election, a pro-rata credit may be issued as a service credit for future invoices in lieu of a cash refund. Service credits do not expire for 24 months.
